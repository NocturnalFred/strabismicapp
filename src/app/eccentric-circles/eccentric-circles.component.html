Circles move a particular distance per interval. The shorter the interval the faster the movement. Shortest possible interval should be 200ms. The longest interval should be no more than 1000ms to avoid apparent freezing of the game. The game is not actually frozen. instead what happens is if you put in 10000ms that is equivalent to saying update every 10 seconds. It will take at least 10 seconds to update the page.
